# Calculator Cove: Gameplay Modes and Multiplayer Plan

## New gameplay types

### Connect the dots

- Classic format.
- Etching shapes into the sand like tic tac toe.
- First to four wins.

### Breakwater

- Time limit objective: be the only one with a 4-long wall.
- Alternative concept: walls protect something, then at time lapse a wave washes away some or all player moves until a win condition is met.

### Shell toss

- Players throw pearls into open seashells.
- Selecting the grid point calibrates an X/Y throw power gauge slider (like a golfing video game) to aim at the selected shell.
- If they miss, the game skips their turn.

### Treasure Hunt

- Metal detector game mode.
- The player picks a tile to scan and gets a weak/strong detector reading based on:
  - proximity, and
  - item characteristics.
- To dig in the selected tile, the player must commit to skipping their next turn.
- Item design:
  - tiny but valuable items,
  - large pieces of garbage,
  - large treasures,
  - small pieces of garbage.
- Some spaces contain nothing but may still produce a light reading if adjacent to a tile with an item.
- Each item has a point value.
- Player with the most points at the end wins.

### Swarming seagulls

- Single-player mode.
- Player has free control over both arrows for selecting products.
- Player must shoo seagulls assigned to certain products by selecting their factors.
- Time pressure: if too slow, seagulls steal all the fries.

### Battleship

- Needs fixes.
- Expanded grid can still be explored after stability fixes.

### Go experiment

- Go-like gameplay on expanded grid as an experiment.

### Hermit crab habitat

- On a 12x12 grid, players use the factor selection tool to play a Go-style game.
- Each player controls one hermit crab color.
- Crabs switch shells according to Go-style rules.

### Deep sea fishing

- Players select and move their fishing line searching for big catches.
- After a set time or turn limit, player with the biggest fish wins.
- Skill element ideas:
  - fish tiles may be visually distinct (for example, darker),
  - fish move one tile vertically or horizontally after every turn.

## Access model

- Some game modes are unlockable.
- Some game modes are paywalled.

## Leaderboards and profile stats

- Leaderboard stats are stored to a player's profile in:
  - win,
  - loss,
  - draw,
  - quit format.

## Match types

- Players can play:
  - against a computer locally,
  - local multiplayer,
  - online multiplayer.

## Online multiplayer communication controls

- Online multiplayer includes strictly controlled communication.
- Players can select only from a limited emoji set.
- Emoji displays when a player clicks the emoji.
